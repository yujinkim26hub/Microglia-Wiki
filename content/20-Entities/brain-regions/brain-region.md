# Brain region

> [!abstract] In one line
> Anatomical subdivisions of the brain across which [[microglia]] show distinct density, morphology, and [[cell state|states]].

## Overview
This is the index page for brain regions. [[Microglia]] are regionally heterogeneous — e.g., white matter vs. gray matter, cerebellum, hippocampus, cortex, basal ganglia — with region-specific transcriptomes and disease vulnerability. Individual region pages can be added under `20-Entities/brain-regions/` as needed.

## Relevance to microglia
- Regional microglial heterogeneity interacts with [[development stage]] to produce spatiotemporal diversity.
- Selective regional vulnerability is a hallmark of [[neurodegenerative disease]].
- Resolved by [[spatial transcriptomics]] and [[brain single-cell atlas|atlases]].
