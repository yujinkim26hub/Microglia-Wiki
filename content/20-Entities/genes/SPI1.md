# SPI1

> [!abstract] In one line
> The myeloid master transcription factor PU.1 — a conserved regulator of [[microglia]] identity and a nominated regulator of [[Alzheimer's disease]] risk.

## Overview
*SPI1* encodes **PU.1**, an ETS-family transcription factor essential for myeloid/microglial development and identity ([[gene interaction|gene-regulatory networks]]). It is conserved across species as a core microglial regulator and is implicated as an [[Alzheimer's disease]] risk gene.

## Relevance to microglia
- Conserved microglial lineage regulator ([[shimizu-2025-microglia-evolution]]).
- Nominated as a **key regulator of microglial expression and AD risk** in the human microglia regulome ([[kosoy-2022-microglia-regulome]]).
- A hub for microglial [[gene interaction|regulatory networks]] and candidate [[therapeutic targets|therapeutic modulation]].
