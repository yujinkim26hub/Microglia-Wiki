# BIN1

> [!abstract] In one line
> The second-strongest common genetic risk locus for late-onset [[Alzheimer's disease]], with proposed microglial/myeloid regulatory effects.

## Overview
*BIN1* (bridging integrator 1) is a major late-onset [[Alzheimer's disease]] risk gene. Fine-mapping and functional work implicate noncoding regulatory variants with effects in [[microglia|microglial]]/myeloid cells, among other cell types ([[gene relationship]]).

## Relevance to microglia
- A candidate causal AD variant at *BIN1* was fine-mapped in a microglia-like (iPSC-macrophage) model ([[young-2021-microglia-transcriptional-map]]).
- Prioritized as a likely causal AD gene by integrative GWAS analysis ([[schwartzentruber-2021-ad-gwas-finemapping]]).
