# APOE

> [!abstract] In one line
> The strongest common genetic risk factor for late-onset [[Alzheimer's disease]]; a key lipid-transport gene upregulated in [[disease-associated microglia]].

## Overview
*APOE* encodes apolipoprotein E, central to lipid transport. The ε4 allele markedly increases late-onset AD risk while ε2 is protective. In brain, APOE is expressed by astrocytes and disease-associated [[microglia]].

## Relevance to microglia
- *Apoe* is a hallmark of the [[disease-associated microglia]] program ([[gene relationship]]).
- Interacts with [[TREM2]] signaling in microglial lipid handling ([[gene interaction]]).
- Influences [[neuroinflammation]] and is a [[therapeutic targets|therapeutic]] and [[biomarkers|biomarker]] focus.
