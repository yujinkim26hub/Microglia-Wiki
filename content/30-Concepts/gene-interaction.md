# Gene interaction

> [!abstract] In one line
> Functional or physical interplay between genes/products — regulatory networks, co-expression, epistasis, and pathways.

## Definition
Gene interactions describe how genes act together: transcriptional regulation (gene regulatory networks), protein–protein interactions, co-expression modules, and genetic epistasis. Distinct from [[gene relationship|gene–phenotype relationships]].

## Why it matters for microglia / neurological disease
Microglial [[cell state|states]] and [[microglia activation|activation]] programs are coordinated by regulatory networks; mapping these networks links risk genes to mechanism and to [[therapeutic targets]].

## Key points
- Inferred via GRN methods (e.g., SCENIC), co-expression (WGCNA), and PPI databases.
- Multi-modal evidence ([[multi-omics]]) strengthens inferred networks.
- Complements [[cell-cell interaction|intercellular]] signaling analysis.
