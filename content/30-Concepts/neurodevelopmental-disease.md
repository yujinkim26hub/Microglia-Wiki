# Neurodevelopmental disease

> [!abstract] In one line
> Disorders arising from atypical brain development, including autism spectrum disorder, intellectual disability, and schizophrenia-spectrum risk.

## Definition
Neurodevelopmental diseases originate during prenatal/postnatal brain [[development stage|development]] and affect cognition, communication, and behavior — e.g., autism spectrum disorder (ASD), intellectual disability, ADHD, and developmental components of schizophrenia.

## Why it matters for microglia / neurological disease
[[Microglia]] shape development via synaptic pruning and [[cell-cell interaction]]; disrupted microglial function during critical [[development stage|windows]] is linked to neurodevelopmental risk and overlaps with [[neuropsychiatric disease]].

## Key points
- Microglial synaptic pruning (e.g., complement-mediated) is developmentally critical.
- Strongly [[development stage|timing]]- and [[brain region|region]]-dependent.
