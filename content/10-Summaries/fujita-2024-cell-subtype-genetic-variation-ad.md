## Core question
How does genetic variation affect RNA expression at the level of specific brain **cell types and subtypes** ([[cell state]]) in the aged/[[Alzheimer's disease]] brain ([[gene relationship]])?

## Prior studies
Bulk and cell-type eQTLs exist, but fine-grained **cell-subtype** eQTLs across many donors were limited.

## Limitations of prior studies
Coarse cell-type resolution misses subtype-specific regulatory effects relevant to disease.

## What this study did
Generated large-scale single-nucleus RNA-seq from aged human neocortex and mapped cis-eQTLs across cell types and subtypes.

## Main results of this study
- snRNA-seq of neocortex from **424 individuals** of advanced age; **~1.5 million transcriptomes**.
- Assessed cis genetic effects across **7 cell types and 64 cell subtypes**.
- Identified **10,004 eGenes at cell-type level** and **8,099 at cell-subtype level** ([[gene relationship]]), including [[microglia|microglial]] effects relevant to AD.
