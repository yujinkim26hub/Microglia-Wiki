## Abstract
Introduces sc-linker, integrating scRNA-seq-derived gene programs, enhancer–gene maps and GWAS summary statistics to infer disease-critical cell types and cellular processes; recapitulates known biology and highlights cell–disease relationships (e.g. complement-cascade process in multiple sclerosis). *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
