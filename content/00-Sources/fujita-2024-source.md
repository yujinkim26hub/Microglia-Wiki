# Source: Fujita et al. (2024)

- **Title:** Cell subtype-specific effects of genetic variation in the Alzheimer's disease brain
- **Journal:** Nature Genetics — 56(4):605–614 (2024)
- **DOI:** [10.1038/s41588-024-01685-y](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-024-01685-y)
- **PMID:** [38514782](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38514782/) · **PMCID:** [PMC12288883](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12288883/)
- **Publisher link:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-024-01685-y

## Abstract
Single-nucleus RNA-seq from the neocortex of 424 aged individuals (~1.5 million transcriptomes) assesses cis genetic effects across 7 cell types and 64 subtypes, identifying 10,004 eGenes at cell-type level and 8,099 at cell-subtype level, including effects relevant to AD. *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
