# Source: Young et al. (2021)

- **Title:** A map of transcriptional heterogeneity and regulatory variation in human microglia
- **Journal:** Nature Genetics — 53(6):861–868 (2021)
- **DOI:** [10.1038/s41588-021-00875-2](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-021-00875-2)
- **PMCID:** [PMC7610960](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7610960/)
- **Publisher link:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-021-00875-2

## Abstract
Single-cell and bulk RNA-seq of primary human microglia from 141 neurosurgery patients characterizes age/sex/pathology-related heterogeneity, maps microglia eQTLs, and fine-maps an AD candidate causal variant at BIN1 using an iPSC-derived macrophage model. *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
