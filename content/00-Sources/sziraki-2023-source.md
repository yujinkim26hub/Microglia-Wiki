## Abstract
EasySci single-cell combinatorial-indexing profiles ~1.5M single-cell transcriptomes and ~400k chromatin-accessibility profiles across aging mouse brains (>300 subtypes) plus 118,240 human brain transcriptomes, revealing rare aging-dynamic cell types and cell/region-specific signatures of AD pathogenesis. *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
