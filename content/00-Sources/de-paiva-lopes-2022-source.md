- **Title:** Genetic analysis of the human microglial transcriptome across brain regions, aging and disease pathologies
- **Journal:** Nature Genetics — 54(1):4–17 (Jan 2022)
- **DOI:** [10.1038/s41588-021-00976-y](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-021-00976-y)

## Abstract
Bulk transcriptomic analysis of 255 primary human microglial samples from 100 individuals across multiple brain regions; identifies brain region, age, sex and disease as sources of microglial heterogeneity and maps expression/splicing QTLs implicating variants in neurological disease. *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
