# Source: Schwartzentruber et al. (2021)

- **Title:** Genome-wide meta-analysis, fine-mapping and integrative prioritization implicate new Alzheimer's disease risk genes
- **Journal:** Nature Genetics — 53(3):392–402 (2021)
- **DOI:** [10.1038/s41588-020-00776-w](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-020-00776-w)

- **Publisher link:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-020-00776-w

## Abstract
Updated AD GWAS meta-analysis identifies 37 risk loci (new: CCDC6, TSPAN14, NCK2, SPRED2); three fine-mapping methods nominate 21 high-probability causal SNPs; colocalization across 109 eQTL datasets plus network/tissue prioritization converges on candidate causal genes (incl. BIN1, APH1B, PTK2B, PILRA, CASS4). *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
