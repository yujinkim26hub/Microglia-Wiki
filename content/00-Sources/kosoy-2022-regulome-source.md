# Source: Kosoy et al. (2022) — microglia regulome

- **Title:** Genetics of the human microglia regulome refines Alzheimer's disease risk loci
- **Journal:** Nature Genetics — 54(8):1145–1154 (2022)
- **DOI:** [10.1038/s41588-022-01149-1](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-022-01149-1)
- **PMID:** [35931864](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35931864/) · **PMCID:** [PMC9388367](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9388367/)
- **Publisher link:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-022-01149-1

## Abstract
Transcriptome + chromatin-accessibility profiling of primary human microglia from 150 donors maps enhancer–promoter interactions and refines 21 AD risk loci (18 to a single gene; new candidates KCNN4, FIBP, LRRC25), nominating SPI1 as a key microglial regulator of AD risk. *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
