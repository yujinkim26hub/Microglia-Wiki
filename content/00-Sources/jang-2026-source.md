## Abstract
"SingleBrain" meta-analysis integrating snRNA-seq + genotype data from four cohorts (~5.8 million nuclei, 983 individuals of European ancestry); maps cis- and trans-eQTLs across major brain cell types/subtypes (incl. disease- and sex-specific), and uses colocalization and Mendelian randomization to nominate genes mediating brain-disorder risk. *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
