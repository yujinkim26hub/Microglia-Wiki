- **Title:** Human microglia show unique transcriptional changes in Alzheimer's disease

## Abstract
Single-nucleus RNA-seq of microglia from 12 AD and 10 control human dorsolateral prefrontal cortices (>60 yr) identifies 10 microglia clusters (homeostatic, senescent, inflammatory and others) and microglial phenotypes more prevalent in AD. *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
