# Source: Yang et al. (2023)

- **Title:** Functional characterization of Alzheimer's disease genetic variants in microglia
- **Journal:** Nature Genetics — 55(10):1735–1744 (2023)
- **DOI:** [10.1038/s41588-023-01506-8](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-023-01506-8)
- **PMID:** [37735198](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37735198/)
- **Publisher link:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-023-01506-8

## Abstract
Microglia candidate cis-regulatory elements (cCREs) show the strongest AD-heritability enrichment among brain cell types. Integrating microglia-specific 3D epigenome annotation prioritizes 308 new AD risk variants at 181 cCREs; single-cell CRISPRi links variants to target genes (e.g. rs7922621→TSPAN14, affecting ADAM10 and sTREM2 shedding). *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
