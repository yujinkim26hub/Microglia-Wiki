# Source: Clarence et al. (2025)

- **Title:** Multiomic single-cell profiling identifies critical regulators of postnatal brain development
- **Journal:** Nature Genetics — 57(3):591–603 (2025)
- **DOI:** [10.1038/s41588-025-02083-8](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-025-02083-8)
- **PMID:** [39962241](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39962241/)
- **Publisher link:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-025-02083-8

## Abstract
Joint snRNA-seq + snATAC-seq of 101,924 nuclei from 4 brain regions across 10 donors and 5 postnatal stages; enhancer-based gene regulatory networks identify cell-type-specific developmental regulators and connect 2,318 cell-specific loci to 1,149 genes across 10 brain disorders (55 genes affecting multiple phenotypes). *(Paraphrased from the published abstract.)*
